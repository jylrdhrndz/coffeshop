    About Jylly Coffee & Our University

    Our Story

    Founded in 2023, Jylly Coffee began as a humble coffee cart inspired by the rich university culture of thought, innovation, and community. We believe in the magic that a great cup of coffee can spark from all-night study sessions to early morning brainstorming.

    University Information

    We proudly partner with our local university to provide students and faculty with high-quality, ethically sourced coffee. The university emphasizes sustainable practices and community driven projects.

    Our Core Values:

    • Sustainability and Ethical Sourcing
    • Quality and Craftsmanship
    • Community Engagement
    • Innovation and Education
